Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I request equipment?
A: Submit a ticket in the internal service desk portal and your manager will approve it.

Q: Is there a probation period?
A: Yes. New employees have a probation period of six months.

Q: Can I carry over vacation days?
A: Yes, but only until March 31 of the next year according to company policy.

Q: Where can I find onboarding material?
A: The onboarding guide is available in the internal knowledge base.
