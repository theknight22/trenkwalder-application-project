# Company Policy Overview

## Working Hours
Employees work 40 hours per week, usually from Monday to Friday.
Core collaboration hours are from 10:00 to 15:00.

## Remote Work
Team members may work remotely up to three days per week.
Any exception should be approved by the direct manager.

## Vacation Rules
Vacation requests should be submitted at least 10 business days in advance.
Unused vacation days can be carried over only until March 31 of the following year.

## Sick Leave
Employees should notify their manager as soon as possible on the first day of sickness.
Medical confirmation is required after the third consecutive sick day.
